Paragraphs and fancy texts

Paragraphs are nice and easy, <p>*content*</p> will make you a paragraph, you can use other tags within paragraphs as Well like strong or em tags, <strong>strong</strong> makes things bold and <em>em</em> makes things italic both of are used for screen readers to know what to emphasize while reading the screen to disabled users.

Fancy Tricks

"< and >" are html specific so to type them out you will need to use >/lt; gt or lt for greater than or less than. But say you wanna add a new line without making a new paragraph you can't just hit enter because html doesnt care about that, you need to use <br> at the end of the line, you don't have to have a closing tag for this either. Want a tab? This one is a little weird <pre>*Tab* *content*</pre> will make the spacing you see there but you can't forget to hit TAB.
